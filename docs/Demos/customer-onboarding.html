Customer Onboarding Demo

Tags: Demo Onboarding Customer Robot Purchase

This demo walkthrough shows the full customer onboarding flow: signing up, verification, wallet setup, robot purchase, activation, and dashboard redirection.

Demo Video​

Process Overview​

This demonstration covers:

  1. Sign up and personal information
  2. Email verification and password setup
  3. Profile completion vs skipping
  4. Full onboarding (address, ID, SSN, selfie, wallet)
  5. Robot purchasing and checkout
  6. Robot activation and configuration
  7. Final redirection to Dashboard home

Step-by-Step Process​

1. Sign Up​

  • On the Sign Up page, enter your phone number and click Continue.
  • You are redirected to the Personal Information form.

2. Personal Information​

  • Enter Full Name, Date of Birth, and Email Address.
  • Tick Terms of Service and Privacy Policy checkboxes.
  • Click Sign Up.

3. Email Verification (OTP)​

  • You'll be taken to the Verify Email Address screen.
  • Enter the 4-digit OTP sent to your email and click Verify.
  • You can also click Resend to receive a new OTP.

4. Set Password​

  • On the Set Password form, enter Password and Confirm Password.
  • Click Confirm.
  • After a successful sign-up, a Congratulations popup is shown. Click Next.
  • You're redirected to the Sign In screen. Enter your password and click Sign In.

5. Complete Profile Prompt​

  • After sign in, a Complete Your Profile dialog appears.
  • You can choose Complete Profile or Skip for now:
    • Skip redirects to the Dashboard but limits some features.
    • You can always complete your profile later in Account Settings.

6. Full Onboarding (Complete Profile)​

If you click Complete Profile, you are redirected to the onboarding flow showing steps on the left:

  • Address Details
  • Driver's License
  • Social Security Number
  • Headshot
  • Wallet Setup
  • Review Provided Information
  • Agree to Background Check

Address Details​

  • Fill in Address Line, City, State, and Zip Code.
  • Click Next.

Driver's License Upload​

  • Upload your driver's license by clicking the upload box and selecting the file.
  • Click Upload, then Next after a successful upload.

Social Security Number​

  • Enter your SSN (used for payments and background check).
  • Click Next.

Headshot Upload​

  • Upload a clear headshot (similar process to driver's license).
  • Click Next.

Wallet Setup​

  • Add a payment method using the Add Payment Method form.
  • Choose between Bank Account and Card tabs.
    • Bank: Account Holder Name, Account Number, Routing Number
    • Card: Card Number, CVC, Expiration Date, Cardholder Name, Country, Zip Code
  • After submission, view My Payment Methods and click Next.
  • Configure Automatic Cash Out Options (Daily or Weekly) and click Save.
  • You're redirected to the Review Provided Information screen.

Review Provided Information​

  • Check which items are labeled Added or Not Added.
  • Click Next.

Background Check Consent​

  • On the Background Check window, click I authorize and agree to start the background check.

7. Robot Purchase & Add Flow​

After onboarding, you are guided to Robot Purchasing and Adding screens:

  • Choose Buy a Robot or Add my robot.
  • If you select Buy a Robot, browse available robots with type, price, and stock availability.
  • Click a robot to view details (battery life, weight capacity, distance per charge, reviews, Q&A) and click Buy Now.

Shipping & Checkout​

  • Enter shipping details: Full Name, Phone, Address Line, City, State, Zip Code.
  • Continue to the Checkout screen and confirm order summary and total price.

Purchase Payment​

  • Use the Add Payment Method form for the purchase (separate from wallet/cash-out methods).
  • Enter payment details and click Pay.

Order Tracking​

  • After payment, you're redirected to the Order Tracking screen with Order ID and summary.
  • Use the Activate Robot link once your robot arrives.

8. Robot Activation​

  • On the Robot Activation screen, click Continue and follow steps:
    • Power your robot
    • Initiate pairing
    • Enter serial number
    • Configure robot settings (name, working hours, max travel distance, max charge level, order acceptance mode)
  • Click Confirm to finish activation.

9. Final Completion​

  • After successful activation, a Congratulations message appears with a Navigate to Home button.
  • Click it to return to the Dashboard Home.

Key Features Demonstrated​

  • Seamless onboarding flow from sign-up to robot activation
  • In-app verification and wallet setup integrated with payments
  • Robot purchasing and activation integrated into onboarding
  • Distinct payment flows for purchases vs wallet/cash-out

Important Notes​

  • Skipping profile completion limits some features but allows dashboard access
  • Verification and background checks are required to enable full platform features
  • Robot purchase payments are separate from wallet/cash-out methods
